Industrial UI

2020 — Led the development of an efficient and adaptable UX and UI design for an industrial system, focusing on a modular grid system and intuitive iconography.

As part of an industrial system design project, I spearheaded the development of User Experience and graphic interface. My strategy was grounded in the deployment of a logical and modular grid system to manage spaces, factoring in technical limitations such as display resolution and client needs.

Leveraging a foundational grid facilitated the creation of a versatile interface, providing the client with the ability to assemble it in a variety of ways tailored to the specific application of each machine.

Beyond UX, I committed to crafting a robust and clear UI. Keeping the needs of the operators and client at the forefront, the outcome was an efficient graphic interface that continues to earn appreciation in daily usage.
