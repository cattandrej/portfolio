Bio

My name is Andrej Cattaneo, I was born in November 1993 and I studied Communication Design at the Politecnico di Milano.

In the Faculty of Design I managed to combine my interest in technology (especially information technology) with creativity and experimentation. 3D modeling, rendering, animation and Physical Computing objects are some of the interests I have cultivated by developing projects together with other colleagues, both for educational reasons and for personal interest.
